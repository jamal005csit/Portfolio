🏫 About E-JUST

Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST) is a research-driven institution in Borg El Arab, Alexandria, founded through Egyptian–Japanese collaboration. The university is renowned for its rigorous academic system, project-based learning approach, and strong emphasis on innovation, engineering, and advanced technology.
